FAQs

What types of canvas art do you sell?

We offer ready-made designs, limited editions, and custom-printed canvas art in various sizes and styles.

Do the colors look the same in real life as on screen?

We ensure sellers provide high-quality and faithful examplary images. Any untruthful advertisement will be met with proper penalties as per our terms and conditions

How long does shipping take?

Most orders are processed within 2–3 business days. Delivery typically takes 5–10 business days depending on your location and the seller.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide! Shipping costs and delivery times vary by region.

How is the artwork packaged?

We carefully wrap each canvas in protective layers and ship it in sturdy, eco-friendly packaging to prevent damage.

Can I track my order?

Absolutely. Once your order ships, you’ll receive an email with a tracking link.
